mario mandzukic has denied claims real madrid defender daniel carvajal bit him during tuesday 's champions league quarter-final match .
mandzukic and carvajal were engaged in a physical battle throughout the goalless first leg of the quarter-final tie at the vicente calderon stadium .
after the contest , carvajal was asked by the spanish media if he had bitten mandzukic after pictures showed carvajal 's mouth close to the forearm of mandzukic .
