dalian wanda group have formalised their purchase of 20 per cent of spanish champions atletico madrid .
the purchase was formalised at an extraordinary general meeting on tuesday where the legal team representing wanda madrid investment signed off on the purchase of 726,707 shares for $ 45million -lrb- # 32.8 m -rrb- .
the deal was initially announced in january but two egms were needed in order to ratify the deal .
